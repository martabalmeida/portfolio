A Arte da Mesa

"A Arte da Mesa" is a modern take on table etiquette, designed to make the subject engaging and accessible. The book features a revamped layout with contemporary design elements and clear, stylish graphics that illustrate proper dining behaviors. By combining user-friendly visuals with practical tips, it transforms the traditional guide into a visually appealing resource. Learning about table manners has never been this enjoyable or easy to understand.

SOLUTION

A design focused on modernizing the traditional etiquette book, using clean, stylish graphics and an intuitive layout to make the content engaging and accessible.

Brand Identity, Brand Strategy, Cover Design, Layout Design, Website
