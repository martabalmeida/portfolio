MARIANA & FELIPE'S WEDDING

A summer wedding in Monte Alegre called for a visual identity that captured the elegance, intimacy, and laid-back nature of the celebration. The goal was to translate the couple's story into a cohesive visual system that felt both personal and timeless.

SOLUTION

The design features delicate, hand-drawn illustrations of the venue, paired with a soft watercolor palette to evoke warmth and charm. From the invitation and wine labels to menus and seating plans, every piece was crafted to contribute to a unified and emotional visual experience.

SCOPE

Brand Identity, Graphic Design, Illustration, Print Design
