Underground

"Underground" celebrates British design, highlighting creators who rose from underground movements into lasting global influence. The inaugural issue honors Peter Saville, famed for iconic album covers for "Joy Division" and "New Order", capturing Manchester's post-industrial spirit.

Beyond music, Saville's work in fashion with brands like Burberry further expanded his impact. The magazine also shows a new wave of British designers, redefining visual identity with authenticity and innovation and continuing the country's creative legacy to inspire future generations.

THE PROJECT

This magazine pays tribute to the legacy of British underground designers, with a special focus on Peter Saville. Known for his iconic album covers for Joy Division and New Order, Saville's minimalist yet emotionally charged aesthetic helped define a generation of music and design. His ability to merge cultural commentary with visual innovation inspired the tone and direction of Underground. The editorial design mirrors his bold approach—clean, provocative, and timeless.

SCOPE

Editorial Design, Layout Design, Typography, Concept Development
